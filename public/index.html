The Digital Malaise

The SE Framework targets the Process tier of organisations with their business processes, subject areas and organisational structures. The framework guides and supports domain experts and all other kinds of roles (incl. business partners and customers) in understanding current solutions and in designing the most effective and efficient processes. Software specialists are an important user group of the process models created with the SE Framework, but they are merely consulted in their creation.
